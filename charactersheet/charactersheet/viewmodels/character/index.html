Ability Scores, Savings Throw, and Other Stats
Click on your Ability Scores to add or edit them. Savings Throw modifiers automatically include your Ability Score modifier and Proficiency Bonus.

Skills, Feats, Features, Traits, and Proficiencies

Spell Slots

Spell Stats

Spells
You can sort your spells by clicking on the headers. View the bottom of the list for a count of your prepared spells.
Weapons
A weapon's To Hit bonus is automatically calculated based on your Strength or Dexterity, proficiency bonus, magical modifier, and property. Use the To Hit Modifier to adjust for features, feats, or special cases.
Armor
Armor Class calculations are based on your equipped Armor, Shield, Magical Modifiers, and Dexterity. Be sure to Don your armor and/or shield so your AC will be calculated.
