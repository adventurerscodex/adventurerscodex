Hit Points and Hit Dice
Click on your max Hit Points to increase them when you level up. Click on a die icon to track expended hit dice.
Ability Scores, Savings Throw, and Other Stats
Click on your Ability Scores to add or edit them. Savings Throw modifiers automatically include your Ability Score modifier and Proficiency Bonus. Your Passive Wisdom is also calculated automatically based on your Wisdom.
Daily Features
These are features who's charges are restored whenever you take a short or long rest; for example, Dragon Breath.
Skills, Feats, and Features
Your Skills take into account your Ability Score modifiers and Proficiency Bonus.
Spell Slots
These slots are automatically refilled after a long rest. Click on a slot to edit it.
Spells
You can sort your spells by clicking on the headers. View the bottom of the list for a count of your prepared spells.
Weapons
A weapon's To Hit bonus is automatically calculated based on your Strength, Dexterity and your weapon's magical modifier and property.
Armor
Your Armor doesn't automatically influence your Armor Class...yet.
