Hit Points and Hit Dice
Click on your Hit Points, when you level up, to increase them. Reminder: Hit Dice are based on your level: one dice per level.
Ability Scores, Saving Throws, and Other Stats
Click on your Ability Scores to edit them. Saving Throw modifiers automatically include your Ability Score modifier and Proficiency Bonus. Your Passive Wisdom is also calculated automatically based on your Wisdom.
Daily Features
These are features who's charges are restored whenever you take a short or long rest; for example, Dragon Breath.
Skills, Feats, and Features
Your Skills take into account your Ability Score modifiers and Proficiency Bonus.
Spell Slots
These slots are automatically refilled after a long rest. Click on a slot to edit it.
Spells
Check the bottom of the list for a count of your prepared spells. You can sort your spells by clicking on the headers.
Weapons
A weapon's To Hit bonus is automatically calculated based on its type.
Armor
